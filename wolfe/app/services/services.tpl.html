Things wear out. Brake pads, tyres, chains etc. but we can replace them. We offer a range of replacement parts to suit your pocket. Our many years of experience allow us to undertake any type of repair. We will often undertake repairs that other shops cannot complete.

Cytech

Cytech is the bicycle industry's recognised training and accreditation scheme for bicycle mechanics.

Cytech technical are a set of courses which encompass the basics of cycle mechanics right through to dealing with the most advanced and high-tech components and parts.

Our shop is Cytech accredited. This combined with over 30 years of experience in dealing with both repair and preventative maintenance makes us a one stop shop for cyclists. Our vast knowledge and expertise cover a wide range of repair issues for example;

Headsets

Derailleur Gear Systems

Wheel building

Internal hub gears

Brake systems (cable operated)

Hubs

Wheel truing and spoke replacement
