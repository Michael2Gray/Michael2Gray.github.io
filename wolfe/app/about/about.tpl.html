Welcome

Wolfe Cycles has been keeping the cyclists of Dublin moving since 1984. Our shop has grown from its small beginnings to enable us to cater for our growing customer base.

We are proud of our range of bicycles, parts, clothing and accessories, but the thing that sets us apart from the rest is an unparalleled level of customer service.

We are a Cytech accredited shop. We have a fully equipped workshop employing fully trained, professional bike mechanics.

With a passion for providing excellent customer service, Wolfe Cycles has secured loyal, strong and long lasting relationships with these cyclists. Our website will give you a feel for the range of products and services we offer. In order to best meet your needs please give us a call or pop in.

Women's Cycling

Here at Wolfe Cycle we have a dedicated women’s section. Men and women are built differently, so their bikes need to be built to accommodate those differences. The differences are mostly seen in the frame’s geometry and the bikes contact points. These changes allow for superior power, increased handling and better balance. We offer a range of specific women’s Commuter, Hybrid and Racing Bicycles. We also stock cycling clothing, helmets and rain gear.
